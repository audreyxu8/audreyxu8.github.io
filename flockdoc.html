Flocky Documentation

This project started out as an idea for animating Swarm Robotics
to show off how it could be used to form the structures of mideval
castles. I imagined it to be like somebody with a video camera,
filming it come to form.

After conducting this interview I later realized that I had
no passion for the idea I had originally come up with.
The fact that swarm intelligence was primarily inspired by
social insects stuck with me, so the idea then changed to
creating a VR experience in which the user could traverse
around a fantastical land, and on it, were various swarms
that you could observe and become apart of. I used Bored
to Bits Games' flocking algorithmn for Unity as a basis
of this project.

I modelled an "insect" using Blender's sculpting tool and textured it in Substance Painter.
