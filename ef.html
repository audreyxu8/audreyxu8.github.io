Emerging Future

This site specific intervention took place on New York City water transportation specificially the MTA ferry and the Staten Island ferry. We had a lot of re-iterating to do for this particular project as we faced difficult constraints. Through designing for this site, we landed on our final idea: projecting images and videos on various parts of the ferry. The physicality of the previous projects were what was difficult to get around so we thouhgt that in doing something with no physicality would be the most effective move.
