The Friend Network

An interactive webpage that will allow users to navigate
through a series of characters and environments I create
in the friendship network. The purpose is for anyone
and everyone to talk about the unapparent difficult nature
of friendships and relationships. By hacking my friends
to create an exaggerated visual representation of them, it
poses the question,

Will I ever be happy with what I have?
