Audrey Xu's work consists of 3D animation, modeling,
as well as creating virtual environments using programs
such as Unity. Most of her work is experimental,
discovering the new and unusual forms that play a part
in her final product. This particular process is what she
finds intent in, and motivates her passion to create novel
art. Her inspiration comes from anything fantastical,
exploring the unknown- the weird and making it a reality
through her work.
